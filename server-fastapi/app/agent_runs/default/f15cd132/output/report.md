# What is a Transformer in Deep Learning?

A Transformer is a neural network architecture introduced in the 2017 paper "Attention Is All You Need" by Vaswani et al. It relies primarily on a mechanism called **self-attention** to process input sequences in parallel, rather than sequentially like RNNs or LSTMs. This design makes Transformers highly efficient to train and enables them to capture long-range dependencies in data. Transformers are the foundation of modern large language models like GPT and BERT, and have been extended to vision, audio, and other domains.
